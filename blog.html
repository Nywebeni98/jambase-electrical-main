Founder's Journey

From Vision to Reality

I'm Lisa Msuthu Nywebeni, and my journey to revolutionize the electrical services industry began with a vision to transform our family business through cutting-edge skills and innovative approaches. This transformation was made possible through the exceptional training I received at both UVU Africa and Capaciti.

What started as a traditional family electrical business has evolved into a comprehensive service provider that combines technical excellence with digital innovation. Through collaboration with incredibly talented youth who share the same passion for excellence, we're not just building a business – we're creating a movement that prioritizes safety, quality, and community empowerment.

UVU Africa Journey

Where It All Began

My transformative journey began at UVU Africa in 2023, where I joined the Youth in Business program and enrolled in their comprehensive digital program. I came with a clear mission: to acquire the skills needed to elevate our family business to new heights.

At UVU Africa, I was introduced to an exceptional IT program that proved to be a game-changer. I learned website development, mastered the art of pitching, and gained the confidence that would become the foundation of my entrepreneurial success.

Our family business had always existed, but we were operating in the dark ages of marketing – relying solely on word-of-mouth and basic pamphlets. UVU Africa opened my eyes to the power of digital marketing and gave me skills that complemented my electrical expertise, creating a powerful combination for business growth.
